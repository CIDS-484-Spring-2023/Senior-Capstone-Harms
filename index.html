Customers

The customers of this app would be any person who works out and wants to help out their friends or even just keep track of their own accomplishments. This could also be for anyone just starting out and has friends that are willing to cheer them on along the way ontheir journey to being healthy. This app could also be used by teams or workout groups who send out workout plans and wants to make sure everyone did them on their own time rather than trying to schedule a time for everyone to meet and do it together. Fitness trainers could alos use this app as a way to help clients who might be on vacation, but still wnat to get some exercise in or even strecthing. Needless to say anyone could use this app if they wanted to get healthy, doesn't matter if they beginners or experienced gym bros.
